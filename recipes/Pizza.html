Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat, round base of leavened dough topped with a variety of ingredients.

The traditional pizza base is made from wheat flour and yeast, rolled out thin or thick depending on the style. It is then baked at high temperatures in a wood-fired oven, although other methods are also common.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • yeat
  • TOmoto sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Turn on the fire
  2. Roll out the dough and put it on fire
  3. Add tomato sauce with your seasoning preference and cheese
  4. Put it again with high temperatures
