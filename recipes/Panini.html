Panini

Description

A panini is an Italian-style sandwich that is typically made with a variety of meats, cheeses, vegetables, and condiments, placed between slices of ciabatta, focaccia, or other crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Ciabatta
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Turn on the fire
  2. Make the Bread Crispy
  3. Add all the ingredients
  4. Grill the bread with all elements in it again (to let the cheese melt)
  5. VOila Bon Appetit
