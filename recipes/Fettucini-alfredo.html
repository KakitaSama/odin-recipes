Fettucini Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish made with thick, flat fettuccine noodles tossed in a rich and creamy sauce.

The sauce is traditionally prepared using just a few simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic

  2. Bowl the pasta
